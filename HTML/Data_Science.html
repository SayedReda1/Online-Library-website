Advances in Data Science: Symbolic, Complex and Network Data


Author: Edwin Diday, Rong Guan, Gilbert Saporta, Huiwen Wang

Genre: Computer Science

Published: January 1, 2024

Description:
Data science unifies statistics, data analysis and machine learning to achieve a better understanding of the masses of data which are produced today, and to improve prediction.
Special kinds of data (symbolic, network, complex, compositional) are increasingly frequent in data science.
